Honoring Phethelo Molapo

Phethelo Molapo was a young boy whose life was well lived but robbed by death at an early age. Through the foundation his name will live long and his kind heart will forever be with us through the love that will be shown by the organization.

Through collective action and dedication, we are transforming lives and building a better tomorrow. Together we make a lasting impact.

Together, we support the future—one child at a time
